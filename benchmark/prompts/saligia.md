# SALIGIA Review (No‑Go Patterns)

*Detect anti‑patterns such as `goto`, `any`, and other discouraged practices.*

- Are any `any` types used in TypeScript?
- Is there a `goto` or similar jump statement?
- Are global variables abused?

Score 0–1 where 1 means no anti‑patterns found.
